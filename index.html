Perfect World Computers

At PWC we're passionate about technology and committed to bringing you the best in computer hardware, software, and accessories. Whether you're a seasoned professional, a gaming enthusiast, or just getting started, we have everything you need to power your digital world.

Every big castle was once started with a single block; despise no small beginnings. A little step taken every day builds up the hope of greater accomplishments. We at PWC believe in doing something every day to improve ourselves.

Our humble beginnings date to 2004,  Perfect World was founded by three college friends and started with providing computer services and AMC for corporate clients and local customers. We started selling second hand/used desktops and laptops from 2005.

Headquartered in Bhopal Madhya Pradesh, India, PWC has grown year after year to be a eighty employee company. Our management team with deep domain knowledge, market leading expertise and passion to improve everyday ensures keeping customer value management at the core.

Over the past couple of years we have become one of the leaders amongst leading IT channel distribution partners in Madhya Pradesh and Chhattisgarh. PWC is Lenovo Consumer RD, Lenovo SMB, Logitech Lord partner, Zebronics RD, Quick Heal Distributor, Samsung Monitors Star Partner for Bhopal and upcountry region.

We also have our DELL, LENOVO and ASUS exclusive stores in Bhopal, a retail store in MP Nagar zone 1 Bhopal, one retail store in Indore and Raipur as well. PWC has been catering to the IT channel of Madhya Pradesh , Chhattisgarh and upcountry since a long time now.
